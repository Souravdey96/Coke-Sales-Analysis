# Coke-Sales-Analysis
This project presents a detailed analysis of beverage sales data for 2021.

The goal is to uncover insights on sales trends, regional performance, pricing patterns, and retailer impact to support better business decisions.

The dataset includes fields such as invoice dates, regions, retailers, brands, units sold, prices, total sales, and margins.

Data cleaning steps included:

Standardizing brand, region, and retailer names

Formatting dates into monthâ€“year

Checking for missing or incorrect values

Creating new metrics such as price per unit and price range categories

Analysis techniques:

Pivot tables and Excel formulas for grouping and summarizing data

Visual exploration using charts for monthly, regional, and price-based trends

Key insights:

Sales peak in July and December

West and Northeast regions lead in revenue

Mid-price products drive the highest sales volume

Recommendations:

Focus marketing in peak months

Strengthen underperforming regions

Align pricing and retailer partnerships with seasonal demand
